# About me

I am a Control and Automation Engineer with a passion for machine learning and edge computing. I earned both my B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees from the [Federal University of Santa Catarina (UFSC)](https://en.ufsc.br/), one of the most prestigious engineering schools in South America. I have also spent one year as a visiting student at [Brown University](https://www.brown.edu/), where I worked as a research assistant in the [Laboratory for Engineering Man/Machine Systems (LEMS)](http://vision2.lems.brown.edu/).
<br/><br/>
In my Master’s thesis, I worked on data-driven state estimation strategies for airborne wind energy systems. My work led to publications in prominent venues such as the IEEE TCST, IFAC, AWEC, REDEC, as well as international collaborations.
<br/><br/>
Over the years I have acquired significant software development experience working on several different projects. Recently, I have also had the opportunity to co-found an edge computing infrastructure startup, which got invested by Porsche and Axel-Springer (APX). 
<br/><br/>
I grew up in [Ijuí](https://goo.gl/maps/X5WMck7KNqHFnajb8), a small city in the south of Brazil, but have lived in Porto Alegre, Florianópolis, Providence, Berlin and, more recently, in Luxembourg. In my spare time, I enjoy reading, playing footbal, biking, and programming.
