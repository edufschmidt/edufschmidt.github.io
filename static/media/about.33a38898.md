# About me

I'm an engineer who likes to build automation tools and infrastructure components. I earned both my B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees from the [Federal University of Santa Catarina (UFSC)](https://en.ufsc.br/), one of the most prestigious engineering schools in South America. I have also spent some time abroad as a visiting student at [Brown University](https://www.brown.edu/).
<br/><br/>
I have significant software development experience and worked on several different projects. Recently, I had the opportunity to co-found an edge computing startup that was invested by Porsche and Axel-Springer (APX). 
<br/><br/>
I grew up in [Ijuí](https://goo.gl/maps/X5WMck7KNqHFnajb8), a small city in the south of Brazil, but lived in Porto Alegre, Florianópolis, Providence, Berlin and, more recently, in Luxembourg. In my spare time, I enjoy reading, hanging out with friends, playing footbal, biking, and programming.
